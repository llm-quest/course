## Course!

The LLM Engineer course is a free course that teaches you how to build LLM applications. 
It includes a lot of hands-on exercises and projects to help you learn by doing. 
The course is designed for beginners with no prior experience in LLM. 
If you are new to LLM, this is the place to start!
